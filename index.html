YOU KNOW WHAT?

HIV cases in the Philippines have surged by 676% from 2010 to 2021, marking the fastest-growing epidemic in the Western Pacific region. By 2030, it is projected that the country could have 145,273 cumulative cases if current trends persist. Men who have sex with men (MSM) account for the majority of new infections, highlighting a critical area for intervention. Despite efforts, only 45.5% of patients achieve viral suppression, underscoring challenges in treatment and care.
