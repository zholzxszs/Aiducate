HIV/AIDS Status in the Philippines End of Year 2024

As of December 2024, the Philippines faces a growing HIV/AIDS crisis with about 215,400 PLHIV and an average of 50 new diagnoses daily, mainly among young males. Only about 64% are on Antiretroviral Therapy.

Interconnected Challenges in the Philippines' HIV/AIDS Crisis

The Philippines faces a rapidly growing HIV/AIDS crisis with about 55 new diagnoses daily and only 64% of people living with HIV on antiretroviral therapy. Late detection and infections among young men who have sex with men are major concerns.

HIV BASICS

HIV Testing

Getting tested is a key step in knowing your HIV status.

PrEP

A preventive pill that blocks HIV before it can infect you.

Viral Suppression

Regular HIV medication keeps the virus at very low levels in your body.

Treatment as Prevention

Taking HIV medicine helps both your health and protects your partners.
